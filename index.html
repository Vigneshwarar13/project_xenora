About Us

Xenora

Xenora where creativity, innovation, passion and social responsibility converge. A National Level Tech Fest organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, pulsates with vitality. Texus offers a spectrum of experience from exhilarating 24-Hour Hackathons alongside an array of 30+ technical and non-technical events, green campaign, to blood donation camps contributing to saving lives, every facet resonates with innovation and purpose.

SRM IST

SRM Institute of Science and Technology, India's educational powerhouse with over 20,000+ students, offers diverse programs in engineering, management, medicine, and humanities. Its dynamic curriculum and global collaborations promise top-tier education. SRMIST's impressive placement record, featuring companies like Adobe, speaks to its excellence. Accredited with an 'A++' grade by NAAC, SRMIST fosters innovation and attracts students from across India.

CSE Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, established in 2004, is dedicated to excellence in education and research. Our mission is to provide students cutting-edge knowledge in line with industry standards, collaborate with top academic institutions for R&D, and promote ethical and social responsibility. With a 100% placement record over the past six years, our alumni excel in leading companies, innovative product design, and fundamental research.
